Our Founding History

Founded in 2005 by a team of forward-thinking legal experts, STACKLY was established with a singular goal: to modernize the practice of law. We recognized that that the traditional model often failed to keep pace with the rapid advancements in technology and finance. Our first office in New York quickly became a hub for innovative legal solutions, primarily focusing on complex litigation and emerging market compliance.

Over the past two decades, STACKLY has grown from a boutique practice into a multinational firm, yet we retain the core values of accessibility and dedication. Our growth is a testament to our philosophy of building long-term, strategic partnerships with our clients, guiding them through every legal challenge with precision and foresight.
